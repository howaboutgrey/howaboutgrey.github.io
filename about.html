Hi, I'm Grey

Or you can call me Hoang Anh, I'm a UI/UX designer from Saigon, Vietnam. I'm passionate about solving problems through design thinking. My work is guided by a strong belief in design as a problem-solving tool, as a way of recognizing and forming relationships between ideas and reality.

As a self-taught designer coming from art background, my love of fine art is peppered throughout with the inclusion of multimedia art forms such as illustrations, photo manipulation, and micro interactions. I'm always fascinated with how a creative outlook plays a vital role in life - a great meal, a hit song, an inspired furniture, to a business card that leads to a conversation.

Currently the lead product designer at XPERX AI, I work well on cross-discipline teams, with business leaders, engineers and marketing. I love having ownership over a project and taking it from concept to launch.

Working with small teams have pushed me to be a strong leader, a clear communicator and an advocate for having efficient processes.I like to work with friends and people that are as passionate about what they do as I am and understand design is the ultimate problem-solving tool.
