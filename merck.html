Prediabetes & Diabetes eDA - Merck VN

Time: Oct 2020 - Jan 2021
Deliverables:
  • 100 dynamic slides & 209 sub-content slides
  • Prototypes
  • HTML5
My role:
  • Art direction
  • Communicating with clients
  • Designing the layout
  • Creating prototypes
  • Leading design team

Process:

1. Empathy and Define

The first problem that our client was facing is the content structure of the slides. During my research about eDA, I realized Health Care Professionals (HCPs) have very little time to present the information to doctors.

Heavy load of content makes it even harder for them to get to the points. The second one is navigating, as they using iPad to present, they can only swipe left or right to get to the page they want to showcase, sometimes it just takes forever.

2. Ideate and Prototype

When my team and I walked through the design sprint together, we knew creating a navigation for each project was a must. We put that as our priority and restructured the content based on the navigation our clients approved.
